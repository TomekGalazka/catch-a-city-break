WHY WARSAW?

INVINCIBLE. Nowadays, still breathing with its glamour's past, presently became the cosmopolitan center in Poland, and the former Eastern Europe area. Like in no other Polish city, here in Warsaw, you will find a splendid mosaic of tradition and modernity through its art, architecture, music and cuisine.

CLOSER THAN YOU THINK! Easily reached within 2 hours from all major cities in Europe.

MONEY SAVER. With Poland's favorable exchange rate, your money in Warsaw goes further than in other European cities.

SPECTACULAR CONTRASTS! Warsaw is an exciting city with many faces, from old historical buildings to modern city of polish designers.

WHY KRAKOW?

World Heritage Charm - the CULTURAL CAPITAL of Poland with diverse cultural events including: Ludwig van Beethoven, Summer Jazz, Jewish Culture and Music in Old Krakow International Festivals.

A UNIVERSITY CITY - bustling day and night with its lively pubs, coffee shops and clubs.

Krakow is a HIVE OF ATTRACTIONS of the unique underground world of salt lakes, chambers and monuments in Wieliczka Salt Mine.

LISTED BY UNESCO, often referred as „Little Rome" or „Florence of the north”. City wrapped in legend, where time flows differently.
